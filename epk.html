Biography

2014 was set to be a big year for the Indie music scene as 4 beautifully average looking men from the Highlands of Scotland decided instead of being in the crowd at the bar why not be on the stage and start getting the booze for free? Luckily for us somewhere along the way we four fellas had managed gain some form of musical skill in our chosen instrument and so it was.

After a boozy affair in a local night spot Ben Rait (lead guitarist) and Ben Zebrowski (singer) had a slight altercation resulting in Mr Rait bitting the front man Mr Zebrowski finger all resulting in the band being called Bite Night.

With a whirlwind first year of gigging at venues all over Scotland including festival appearance at Belladrum Tartan heart and great displays at the Ironworks venue Inverness. 2015 looks to be a big year for us and we can't wait to get stuck into it.
