BACKSTORY

2014 was set to be a big year for the Indie music scene as 4 beautifully average looking men from the Highlands of Scotland decided instead of being in the crowd at the bar why not be on the stage and start getting.

Scottish Indie Rockers Bite Night formed in early 2014 and the four piece hit the ground running making it to the final of the Highland battle of the bands in their first three gigs amongst stiff and far more experienced competition.

MUSIC STYLE AND INFLUENCES

The success of that subsequently led to gaining some great support slots namely the bands first appearance at the famous Ironworks venue to support the hotly tipped Palma Violets. From there on in with gigs all over Scotland and a growing confidence the band played to a busy tent at Belladrum Festival, not a bad achievement for band who formed only 8 months prior and surely a highlight of their journey so far.

SOMETHING

The band have now released a 4 track EP of which one of the tracks "Adelaide Eyes" was selected to appear in R2 Magazines un signed compilation cd for January 2015. The boys also made their first music video with some raunchy oiled up wrestling scenes which is sure to turn heads.

2015 already looks set eclipse 2014s achievements with Brew at the Bog festival and a Main Stage slot at Belladrum already in the pipeline for the handsome fellas the future is sure bright.
