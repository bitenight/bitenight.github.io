Biography

How we Came to be

2014 was set to be a big year for the Indie music scene as 4 beautifully average looking men from the Highlands of Scotland decided instead of being in the crowd at the bar why not be on the stage and start getting the booze for free? Luckily for us somewhere along the way we four fellas had managed gain some form of musical skill in our chosen instrument and so it was.

The Sound

The music it self is high tempo uplifting indie rock well suited to a sunny summers day at a music fest or on a dark cold evening in a dimly lit music venue in winter needing warmed up. With catchy rhythm guitar, fast paced hooky lead breaks, flawless beats and fills and some seriously funky bass our bands sound appeals to music lover across the board.

The Name

After a boozy affair in a local night spot Ben Rait (lead guitarist) and Ben Zebrowski (singer) had a slight altercation resulting in Mr Rait biting the front man Mr Zebrowski finger all resulting in the band being called Bite Night.
